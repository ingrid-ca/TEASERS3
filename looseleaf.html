Loose leaf vs. bags

In the US, 65% of all tea comes in tea bags due to its convenience and the ease of use, however loose leaf tea is preferred by tea enthusiasts due to its superior quality.

Tea bags are typically made using finely ground, low-quality tea while loose leaf tea uses higher quality whole tea leaves and when brewed correctly, produces a more complex flavor.

Through the ritual of brewing loose leaf tea, many people develop a deeper personal connection with it.
