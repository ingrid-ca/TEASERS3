Brewing methods

When brewing loose leaf tea, the main factors to consider are steep time, water temperature, and water quality. The main brewing methods include Western, Gongfu, Grandpa, cold-brew, and Matcha styles.

Depending on the brewing style and type of tea, water temperatures will range from 140°F to 212°F and steep times will range anywhere from 30 seconds to 7 minutes. Finding the right balance of these factors along with good quality water will yield the optimal flavor.
